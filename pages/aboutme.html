About Me

Cedar is an Indigenous photographer, videographer, and content creator based on Vancouver Island. They come from the Ditidaht, Ahousaht, and Dzawada'enuxw Nations. Their creative journey started as a photographer for local concerts. They have since expanded to various areas, such as:

  • Live Music
  • Outdoor Adventure
  • Nature - Plant Macro
  • Portrait

About Cedar

Cedar (they/them) is a Queer Indigenous photographer and videographer based on Vancouver Island. They come from the Ditidaht, Ahousaht, and Dzawada'enuxw Nations. Cedar's creative journey started with local concerts and have since expanded to outdoor adventure and recreation for tourism sectors.

t̓aat̓awaʔsiʔ - taatawasi (taa-ta-wa-see) means star in Ditidaht language, which is an homage to Cedar's name of Étoile.
